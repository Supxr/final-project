Click to Show Game Rules

  • Every round, a pair of dice is rolled for each player using the “ROLL” button
  • The sum of the two dice will be the player's round score
  • If any of the two dice on either player's board is a 1 then that player's round score is 0 (eg: if the player rolls a 6 and 1, they get a round score of 0)
  • If the two dice roll the same number, then the numbers are added, then doubled (eg: if the player rolls 2 and 2, they get a score of (2 + 2) * 2 = 8)
  • The round score is added to the total score for three rounds
  • The player with the highest total score after three rounds wins the game
